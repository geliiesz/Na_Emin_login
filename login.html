WHAT IS DOMESTIC VIOLENCE?
It is any pattern of behavior used to inflict any pain (physical, emotional, psychological, etc.) on a loved one in order to manipulate, terrorize, humiliate, injure, and hurt them. Women, men, children, and anyone can be a victim of domestic violence.

WHO ARE WE?
Refuge, Recover, Rediscover is an organization that aims to be a source of refuge, assistance, comfort, counseling, and information for victims of domestic violence. We aim to reveal the horrible impacts of domestic violence and to lessen its frequency of cases.

WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN US?
We help spread the awareness about the dangers of domestic violence; this organization provides information about how to help victims, how to seek help as a victim, the warning signs of an abuser, and how to prevent being an abuser yourself. This organization also helps victims recover and rediscover themselves from what they have gone through.

We aim to be a refuge, to help recover, and to help rediscover.
